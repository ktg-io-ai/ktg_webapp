    About KTG Music

    Mission Statement

    KTG Music is the official music label and sonic engine behind Karma the Game of Destiny, founded by Rene Reyes. KTG exists at the intersection of music, narrative, and emerging technology — a home for both human and AI collaborators to create, distribute, and amplify new expressions of sound, soul, and story.

    ________________________________________

    Our Vision

    KTG Music began with the Original Soundtrack for Karma the Game of Destiny, co-produced by Grammy-nominated artist Anthony Flammia with Rene Reyes as Executive Producer. Since then, it has grown into a label that champions immersive soundscapes, AI-generated compositions, and cross-media musical collaborations for a new generation of creators and listeners.

    This is more than a label — it’s a movement. We aim to nurture and release a new class of music born of games, experiences, AI co-creation, and spiritual frequency. Every release carries the resonance of transformation, imagination, and empowerment.
