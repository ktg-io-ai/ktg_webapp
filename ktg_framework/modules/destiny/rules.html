Rules of the Game

1. Avatar Creation and Management

Multiple Avatars Allowed: Players may create multiple avatars to explore different aspects of their personality and engage with various communities within the game.

Avatar Limits:

  • 6 Avatars: Red Flag Warning - Memory release capabilities become hindered
  • 12 Avatars: Tilted Machine Effect - Complete login prevention and Terms violation

2. Token System Rules

Token Types: 1-Life ($1.99), 3-Life ($3.99), 6-Life ($6.99), 9-Life ($9.99), Diamond ($999.00)

  • All tokens expire after 30 days to encourage active gameplay
  • Tokens are renewable indefinitely with proper application
  • Diamond tokens provide access to certified diamond purchases and special events
  • Token usage is tracked per avatar as "Age of Avatar"

3. Game Registration

Destiny Registration: Uses 3-life token by default, any token type accepted

Chess Registration: 1-life token per AI opponent, free for player-vs-player matches

4. Zap System

The Zap system ensures fair play and prevents harassment:

  • Any size token provides 10 Zaps per 30-day cycle
  • Zapper and zappee never see each other again at wallet level
  • Zappee retains remaining lives to play with other players
  • All zaps are recorded and viewable in player statistics
  • After 10 zaps, use Hide function to remove unwanted profiles

5. Avatar Termination and Data

  • Players may delete avatars and create new ones at any time
  • Avatar termination does not delete wallet history or token records
  • Email/wallet data survives avatar changes for account continuity
  • Players can withdraw tokens, which deletes the memory of those tokens

6. Door System

Avatars must choose a door representing their approach:

  • Blue Door: Values-based connections
  • Yellow Door: Compatibility-focused matching
  • Red Door: Intimacy and deeper connections

7. Public Trust and Violations

Violation Process:

  • 12+ avatar creation triggers automatic violation email
  • Player must argue why restriction is unjustified
  • Case reviewed by Public Trust of the Creators
  • Only 2nd level and above community members may vote
  • Decision is final and binding

8. Fair Play and Conduct

  • Respect all players and maintain appropriate communication
  • Avatar names and taglines must be appropriate for all audiences
  • No harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior
  • Report violations through proper channels
  • Follow community guidelines in all interactions

9. Karma Concept

"Karma the Game of Destiny" - The game believes in redemption and second chances. Actions have consequences, but players can always work toward positive karma through respectful gameplay and community contribution.

Remember: The game is designed for fun, connection, and personal growth.
Play responsibly and treat others as you wish to be treated.
