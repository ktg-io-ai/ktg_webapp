About the Book

This book explores the intricate relationship between artificial intelligence and human intelligence. It delves into the fundamental principles of AI, examining how machines can learn, adapt, and even exhibit creativity. The book also discusses the cognitive processes that underlie human intelligence, drawing parallels and contrasts between the two. Through a combination of theoretical insights and practical examples, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the nature of intelligence and its potential to shape our future.
